## 摘要
供应链韧性是制造业应对外部冲击的关键能力。本文以人工智能与产业技术融合为背景，研究制造业企业如何借助人工智能提升供应链韧性。首先，梳理了供应链韧性的内涵与测度方法。其次，构建了人工智能应用对供应链韧性影响的理论框架。再次，基于企业级面板数据进行了实证检验。研究表明，人工智能应用能够显著提升供应链韧性，且数据要素能力在其中起到正向调节作用。本文的研究为制造业企业智能化转型提供了理论支撑与实践参考。

关键词：人工智能；供应链韧性；制造业；数据要素；实证分析

## ABSTRACT
Supply chain resilience is a key capability for manufacturing enterprises to cope with external shocks. This paper studies how manufacturing enterprises can improve supply chain resilience with artificial intelligence under the background of ai and industrial technology integration. First, the connotation and measurement of supply chain resilience are reviewed. Second, a theoretical framework of the impact of ai application on supply chain resilience is constructed. Third, an empirical test is conducted based on firm level panel data. The results show that ai application can significantly improve supply chain resilience. This study provides theoretical and practical references for the intelligent transformation of manufacturing enterprises.

Keywords：artificial intelligence, supply chain resilience, manufacturing, data element, empirical analysis
